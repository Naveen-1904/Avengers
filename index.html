Avengers


The Avengers are a superhero team, kind of like Earth's mightiest protectors. They come from comics (originally created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963) and have become super famous in movies too. The team is made up of different superheroes who join forces to fight big threats. Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, and Hawkeye are some of the original members. They save the world from aliens, supervillains, and other big problems. There are even Avengers from different realities out there!
